About the Crop:

Tomato - PUSA Hybrid 2 is a high-yielding variety known for its firm, red fruits and resistance to diseases. It thrives in well-drained, loamy soils with a pH of 6.0 to 7.0. The crop requires approximately 500-800 mm of water per acre, which translates to about 1.64 to 2.62 feet. Typically, PUSA Hybrid 2 yields around 25,000 to 35,000 kg of tomatoes per acre. Profit margins are generally favorable, ranging from 20-30%, depending on market prices and production costs.

  • Soil Composition: Well-drained, loamy to sandy loam soils with PH 6.0 to 7.0 .
  • Water need per Acre: 1.64 to 2.62 feet
  • Potential Dangers: Chemical Risk, Soil Degradation, Pest Infestation, Waterlogging
  • Fertilizers: Diammonium Phosphate, Calcium Nitrate, Organic Matter, Potassium, Nitrogen
  • Pesticides: Bacillus thuringiensis ,Thiamethoxam, Acetamiprid
  • Profit Per Acre: ₹5,00,000 to ₹14,00,000
